# Documents

Primary-source material that is **not** a court filing in *Melody v. BCSD* but is part of the documentary record of the Open Records Act dispute that preceded and surrounds the case. Court filings live under [Court Filings]({{ site.baseurl }}/filings/).

This section has two subsections:

## [Pre-Litigation Record]({{ site.baseurl }}/documents/pre-litigation/)

The Georgia Attorney General's two mediation letters, the Georgia DOE response, BCSD's denial letter to a parallel civic-parent requester (Kerry Hatcher), and the Parker Poe ORR cost schedule across the three parallel requester tracks. This is the documentary evidence for the narrative on the [Background]({{ site.baseurl }}/docs/background/) page.

- [BCSD Denial Letter to Hatcher (Mar. 14, 2025)]({{ site.baseurl }}/documents/pre-litigation/bcsd-denial-hatcher/)
- [GA DOE Response Letter (Apr. 14, 2025)]({{ site.baseurl }}/documents/pre-litigation/doe-response/)
- [Parker Poe ORR Cost Schedule]({{ site.baseurl }}/documents/pre-litigation/orr-cost-schedule/)

## [Open Records Requests]({{ site.baseurl }}/documents/orr/)

The parallel ORR record — primarily Kerry Hatcher's civic-parent filings during March 2025, which ran alongside the *Macon Melody*'s journalism-track requests. The JH-settlement ORR at the core of Hatcher's batch asked BCSD for the same settlement records the Melody case now seeks.

- [Requests Overview]({{ site.baseurl }}/documents/orr/)

---

{: .note }
> **Why these documents are here.** The Open Records Act disputes that led to *Melody v. BCSD* did not begin with the complaint on August 4, 2025. They began in July 2024 and ran through the Georgia Attorney General's Open Government Mediation Program, a response from the Georgia Department of Education, and a Parker-Poe-issued refusal pipeline that operated against three different requester categories simultaneously. The [Background]({{ site.baseurl }}/docs/background/) page narrates that sequence. This section hosts the underlying primary sources.
