# Pre-Litigation Record

The documentary record of the ~14-month Open Records dispute that preceded *Melody v. BCSD*. Every document on this list is a primary source. The [Background]({{ site.baseurl }}/docs/background/) page narrates the sequence in plain English; this section hosts the underlying documents.

## Chronological

| Date | Document | Author | Why it matters |
|------|----------|--------|---------------|
| 2024-10-25 | [AG Letter A — Opening]({{ site.baseurl }}/documents/pre-litigation/ag-letter-opening/) | Senior Asst AG Kristen Settlemire | Opens the Corley mediation; reads *Mullins v. City of Griffin* as authority for disclosure. |
| 2025-03-05 | [Parker Poe response — Alex II teacher ORR]({{ site.baseurl }}/documents/pre-litigation/orr-cost-schedule/) (part of the Cost Schedule page) | Sherry H. Culves, Parker Poe | First confirmed Parker Poe ORR touchpoint in the record. $1,590 cost estimate issued to internal-staff requesters using the same exemption basket later used against Corley and Hatcher. |
| 2025-03-14 | [BCSD Denial Letter to Hatcher]({{ site.baseurl }}/documents/pre-litigation/bcsd-denial-hatcher/) | Sherry H. Culves, Parker Poe | Deploys the 34 C.F.R. § 99.3 "requester-knowledge" FERPA theory in writing; concedes the YouTube recording is the only recording of the 7/18/24 meeting. |
| 2025-04-14 | [GA DOE Response Letter]({{ site.baseurl }}/documents/pre-litigation/doe-response/) | Stacey Suber-Drake, GA DOE Chief Legal Officer | Declines state intervention; redirects to AG Open Government Mediation Program. |
| 2025-04-24 | [AG Letter B — Close-Out]({{ site.baseurl }}/documents/pre-litigation/ag-letter-closeout/) | Senior Asst AG Kristen Settlemire | Closes the Corley mediation at impasse; rejects BCSD's reliance on *Mullins*; points to O.C.G.A. § 50-18-73(a) private enforcement. Exhibit F to the Complaint. |
